How to Help

European rabbits in Australia face significant threats from habitat loss, disease, and changing landscapes. You can make a real difference by getting involved — whether through spreading awareness in your community, supporting conservation efforts, or reporting rabbit sightings to help researchers track populations. Every contribution, big or small, helps protect this species for future generations.
